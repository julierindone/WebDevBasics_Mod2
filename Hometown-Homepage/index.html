There's just so much to SEA!

Three attractions that vintage-loving visitors
won't want to miss:

Shop for treasures
on Retro Row

4th street features coffee, casual dining, and more vintage stores than any other spot in the city. Meow has been selling vintage clothing since 1988.

See a movie
at the Art Theatre

Recently restored to its 1930s art-deco splendor, the Art Theatre specializes in documentaries and independent films, and features a wine bar.

Stay on the
RMS Queen Mary

Hunt for ghosts, enjoy a cocktail in the Observation Bar, and watch the sun set from the top deck. The Queen Mary is the perfect homebase for your adventure.
